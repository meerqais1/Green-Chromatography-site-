Eco-Innovative Techniques in HPLC and GLC for Regulatory Excellence

About the Research

This study explores and compares green HPLC and green GLC techniques in pharmaceutical and food industry regulations, with a focus on reducing environmental impact while maintaining analytical integrity.

Objectives

  • Evaluate environmental and analytical performance of green HPLC vs. GLC
  • Analyze industrial case studies implementing green chromatographic methods
  • Support regulatory transitions to sustainable analytical practices

Research Methodology

The study uses a mixed-methods approach, combining performance metrics with qualitative insights from case studies, supported by regulatory documentation and sustainability reports.

Visual Gallery

Key Findings (Expected)

  • Green HPLC offers lower toxicity and solvent waste with strong resolution
  • Micro-GC shows portability and speed advantages in food safety testing
  • Regulatory agencies are gradually aligning with green chemistry principles
